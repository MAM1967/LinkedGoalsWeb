Privacy Policy

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers solely to improve and maintain our Services.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law.

You have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact info@linkedgoals.app for assistance.
